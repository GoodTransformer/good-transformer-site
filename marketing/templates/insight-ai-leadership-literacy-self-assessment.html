The AI Leadership
Literacy Self-Assessment

AI literacy is not knowing every tool. It is the durable judgement that sits above any tool. Score yourself honestly across the five parts below, total it up, and read where you stand.

How to use Score each statement from 1 (not true) to 5 (very true). There are 15 statements, so the total runs from 15 to 75. Add up your score and read your band on page two.

What your score means

15–34

Early

You know AI matters but the judgement is still forming. Start by using one tool on one real task, deliberately.

35–54

Developing

You have real footing in some parts and gaps in others. Your lowest-scoring part is your next move.

55–75

Confident

You can lead AI decisions on judgement, not hype. The work now is keeping it current and spreading it.

Look past the total. Add up each part separately too. The part where you scored lowest, capability, application, evaluation, accountability or adaptation, is the most useful place to spend your next hour. A high total with one weak part is still a real exposure.
