The AI Leadership Readiness Checklist

The biggest AI skills gap is usually at the top. Score your leadership team honestly across the five obligations below, before turning any of this on the staff.

Score each statement from 1 (not true) to 5 (very true) as a leadership team, not as individuals. Fifteen statements, so the total runs 15 to 75. The lowest-scoring area is the obligation to address first.

What your score means

15–34

Absent

The gap is at the top. Start with direction: agree the one sentence before anything else.

35–54

Partial

Some obligations met, others missing. Your lowest area is leaking the most value.

55–75

Ready

Leadership is doing its job. Now keep the rhythm and spread what works.

Read the areas, not just the total. Add up each obligation separately. The lowest one, direction, behaviour, permission, governance or learning, is where the staff are most likely stuck, and it is a leadership fix, not a training one.
