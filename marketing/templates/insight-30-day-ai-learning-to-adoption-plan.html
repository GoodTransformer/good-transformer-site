The 30-Day AI
Learning-to-Adoption Plan

A workshop teaches the tool. This turns it into a changed way of working. Name the one workflow the training is meant to change, then run the four weeks below.

Before week one
The workflow we are changing:
What will be different about it:
Week 1
Learn
  • Run the lesson or workshop on the specific workflow.
  • Capture real examples from your own work, not generic ones.
Owner
Week 2
Apply
  • Use it on real work within days, while it is fresh.
  • Note what worked and where the AI misled you.
Owner
Week 3
Redesign
  • Change the workflow so the new way is the easy way.
  • Move the approval, update the template, set the standing prompt, reassign the check.
Owner
Week 4
Reinforce
  • Share the examples that worked; name a champion.
  • Recognise good use; set the date to review it next.
Owner
Did it stick? (ask at day 30)

Is the new way now the default, or has the team drifted back? If it drifted, the redesign was too light, not the training. Fix the workflow, not the workshop.
