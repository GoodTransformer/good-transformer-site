The AI Agent Job Card

An agent is a delegated role, not a feature. Do not switch one on until every line below is filled. The gaps you cannot complete are precisely your exposure.

1

Purpose

One job, in a sentence. If it needs a paragraph, the scope is too wide.

2

Tasks

The specific things it does. Anything not listed is out of scope.

3

Tools and data

What it can reach. Read access and write access are different jobs.

4

Permitted actions

What it may do on its own. Keep this short and honest.

5

Actions requiring approval

Anything irreversible, costly, or affecting a person.

6

Quality standard

What “good” output is, and how it is checked.

7

Escalation conditions

When it must stop and hand to a human.

8

Logging

What it records, so its actions can be reviewed.

9

Human owner

One named person. Not a team.

10

Shutdown procedure

How to turn it off quickly, and who can. If you cannot say, you are not ready to start.

Completed example · CV-triage agent

A recruitment agency’s triage agent

The same agent, defined. Same technology as an undefined one, completely different risk.

1

Purpose

Triage and summarise new inbound job applications.

2

Tasks

Read each new CV; produce a structured summary against the role’s must-haves.

3

Tools and data

Read-only access to the applications inbox and the role spec. No write access to the ATS.

4

Permitted actions

Draft a summary and a suggested shortlist tier. Nothing leaves the system.

5

Actions requiring approval

Any contact with a candidate. Any rejection. Any write to the ATS.

6

Quality standard

Covers the must-haves, flags gaps, invents no detail. Spot-checked daily by the owner.

7

Escalation conditions

Ambiguous CVs, anything borderline, anything outside the role spec.

8

Logging

Every summary, with its source CV and timestamp, kept for audit.

9

Human owner

Priya Shah, senior consultant.

10

Shutdown procedure

Owner or ops lead disables the workflow in one click. Default to off if anything looks wrong.

Illustrative example, not a specific agency. Notice the pattern: reading is permitted, anything that touches a person or is hard to undo needs approval, and one named human owns it with a way to stop it.
