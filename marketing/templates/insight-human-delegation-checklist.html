The HUMAN Delegation Checklist

AI can inform almost any decision. The question is whether the responsibility can be separated from a person. Tick each box that is true of the decision in front of you.

The decision you are testing
H

HIGH CONSEQUENCE

Serious harm to a person, the business or a client if it goes wrong.

U

UNCLEAR OR CONTESTED

No single right answer; it turns on judgement and competing interests.

M

MORALLY OR RELATIONALLY SIGNIFICANT

It touches how you treat people, or a relationship that matters.

A

ACCOUNTABLE TO LAW, CUSTOMERS OR EMPLOYEES

You would have to stand behind it to a regulator, a court, a client or your staff.

N

NOT READILY REVERSIBLE

A mistake lands and stays; it cannot be quietly undone.

Count the ticks, then read the zone below. The more boxes ticked, the more firmly the decision stays with a person.

Green

0–1 ticks. AI may decide, with light human review. Reversible, low-stakes work.

Amber

2–3 ticks. AI assists, a human decides and signs off. Keep the person in the chair.

Red

4–5 ticks. A human owns it. AI may inform the thinking but never make the call.
